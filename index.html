About Our Solution

CyberByte is a powerful browser extension that protects users from deepfake videos and phishing attempts. Our solution uses advanced AI algorithms to detect manipulated content and suspicious links in real-time, providing immediate warnings to keep you safe online.

Deepfake Detection

Instantly identifies AI-generated or manipulated videos with high accuracy, helping you distinguish between real and fake content.

Phishing Protection

Analyzes links and websites for phishing indicators, preventing you from falling victim to credential theft and scams.

Real-time Alerts

Provides immediate warnings when suspicious content is detected, ensuring you're always aware of potential threats.
